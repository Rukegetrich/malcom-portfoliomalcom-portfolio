Education · Immersive Learning · Africa

Hardware and UX project: a low-cost DIY VR headset designed for underserved Nigerian schools

My Stemscope

Reimagining practical STEM education without requiring a science lab.

Problem

Millions of students across Nigerian public schools lack access to functional science laboratories, making hands-on STEM learning almost impossible in many regions.

Design decision

The original concept was a full virtual lab environment. Research quickly revealed the real challenge wasn't immersion, it was accessibility, affordability, and offline usability.

Role Product Designer Tools Figma, Adobe XD
Process
  • Designed a low-cost DIY VR headset using locally sourced materials
  • Created offline-first learning experiences for low-connectivity environments
  • Iterated prototypes through student and teacher feedback during testing

"Design can create access where infrastructure falls short."
