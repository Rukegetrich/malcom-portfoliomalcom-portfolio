BravePay

Fintech · Mobile Wallet

Mobile-first digital wallet and payment platform built for SMEs in the African market

Senior Product Designer Sketch · Figma · InVision

BravePay needed to help African SMEs send, receive, and verify payments digitally, without the anxiety that most fintech compliance flows create at the exact moment trust is most fragile. The challenge wasn't technical. It was perceptual.

Problem

Compliance-heavy onboarding was colliding with the need to feel simple and welcoming for first-time digital wallet users. Every additional verification step introduced doubt, not security.

What we built

A wallet and KYC flow that surfaced verification requirements only when they became relevant, not as an upfront gate. Compliance happened in the background of the experience, not in front of it.

The hard call

The original KYC flow had nine steps. Testing showed abandonment at step three, not because the steps were unreasonable, but because users couldn't see where it ended. We removed five steps entirely and introduced a single progress marker. The first version of this felt like giving something up. It turned out to be the right call.

+25% Lift in checkout conversion after reducing the verification flow from nine steps to four, and repositioning compliance as context rather than a gate.
